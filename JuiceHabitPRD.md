PRD: The 7-Day Rainbow Challenge \& Spectrum Dashboard

1\. Product Overview

The 7-Day Rainbow Challenge is a time-bound event where users must "Close their Color Rings" every day for one week. The goal is to maximize nutrient diversity by consuming one juice from each primary color group (Green, Orange/Yellow, Red/Purple) throughout the week.

2\. Target User Psychology

•	The Completionist: Wants to see the rings close.

•	The Socialite: Wants to share their vibrant "Rainbow Streak" on Instagram/Fresh Feed.

•	The Identity-Seeker: Wants to feel like "the kind of person who juices."

3\. Feature Specifications

A. The Spectrum Rings Dashboard (Visual Core)

The dashboard is the "Home" screen. It replaces boring lists with three concentric, glowing rings.

Ring	Color Group	Typical Ingredients

Outer Ring	Chlorophyll Green	Kale, Spinach, Cucumber, Celery, Green Apple

Middle Ring	Beta-Carotene Orange	Carrots, Turmeric, Oranges, Ginger, Pineapple

Inner Ring	Antioxidant Red/Purple	Beets, Red Cabbage, Blueberries, Pomegranate

Interaction Design:

•	Empty State: Rings are 10% opaque "ghost" outlines.

•	Progress State: As the user logs a juice, the corresponding color segment fills with a high-saturation, liquid-textured gradient.

•	Completion State: When all three rings are closed, they pulse with a "Prism Glow," and a "Clink Glasses" button appears to share the achievement.

B. The Challenge Logic

•	Duration: 7 Days.

•	Success Metric: Close at least 1 ring per day; "Perfect Rainbow" if all 3 are closed daily.

•	The "Fridge Forager" Integration: The dashboard features a button: "What’s in my fridge?" that filters recipes specifically by the color ring the user has not yet closed today.

4\. User Flow: The "Daily Squeeze"

1\.	Morning Trigger: Notification: "Day 3 of 7. Your Green Ring is waiting. Ready to glow?"

2\.	Dashboard View: User sees their rings. They tap the Orange Ring.

3\.	Recipe Match: App suggests "The Morning Turmeric Wakeup."

4\.	Action: User juices, takes a quick photo within the app.

5\.	The Fill: A liquid animation fills the Orange Ring.

6\.	Social Loop: Post goes to the "Fresh Feed." Friends "Clink" the glass.

5\. Design Layout (UI Mockup Description)

Header: The "Status Bar"

•	Left: "Day 3/7" (Challenge Progress).

•	Right: 🔥 3 (Current Daily Streak).

Center: The Spectrum Rings

•	A minimalist, dark-mode interface to make the juice colors "pop."

•	Center of the Rings: A live "Vitality Score" number that increases as rings close.

•	Visual Detail: The rings should look like they are filled with actual liquid (slight "sloshing" animation when the phone tilts).

Bottom: Action Dock

•	Button 1 (Left): 🥕 Fridge Forager (Input ingredients).

•	Button 2 (Center - Primary): ➕ Log a Juice (Camera/Manual entry).

•	Button 3 (Right): ✨ Daily Affirmation (The quote widget).
